Holy Angel University

In Angeles City, Pampanga, the Philippines, there is a private Catholic university called Holy Angel University. Don Juan Nepomuceno established the institution in 1933 as a high school, which was eventually transformed into a college in 1952. Holy Angel University now provides a huge selection of undergraduate and graduate degrees in a variety of subject areas, including business, education, engineering, the arts, and sciences.

LUID - a Kapampangan word which means “Long Live”. In its 90 years of establishment, Holy Angel University remains to be a pioneer institution in Central Luzon. Having been able to produce globally competent leaders throughout the decades speaks volume about the influence that the university continues to ignite among its students. This signifies the transcending magnificence of Angelites over more than nine decades. Witnessing and experiencing every Angelite and the university itself overcoming the hurdles of time and life, standing still to continue its undying mission and vision. With the gates of HAU being opened once again, celebrating this magnificent and festive event, as we all create our best moments here for 90 years more.
